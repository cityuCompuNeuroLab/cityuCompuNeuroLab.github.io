About

Welcome to the Laboratory for Computational Neuroscience at the City University of Hong Kong (CityU)! The lab is directed by Dr. Rosa Chan in the Associate Professor,
Department of Electrical Engineering.


Our main research activities include:

  • Computational Neuroscience

  • Neural Prosthesis

  • Brain-Computer Interface

  • Bio-Signal Processing
